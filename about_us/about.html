2018

In 2018, Chanakya Dairy undertook a significant expansion to address the escalating demands of the market and embrace evolving technology with a focus on automation. Our milk processing capacity surged to 500,000 liters per day, complemented by a 30-ton capacity for dried products.

2005

After a decade of operation in Khanna City, our company expanded to meet growing demand. We established Chanakya Dairy Products Private Ltd. in Mandi Gobindgarh, spanning 9 acres and boasting a handling capacity of 250,000 liters of milk per day.

2003

Introducing a tantalizing range of treats! From a variety of ice cream flavors and flavored milk to artisanal desserts like Kaju Pinni, Milk Cake, and Khoya, Chanakya Dairy Products Private Ltd. offers a delightful culinary experience that's both indulgent and refined.

2001

Curd, Lassi & Paneer of HF Super After getting a stupendous response from Market for the quality of products, the group expanded its product portfolio in 2001 by bringing out value added products like Curd, Lassi & Paneer under the brand name of HF Super.

1994

In 1994, the seeds of Chanakya Dairy Products Private Ltd. were sown under the banner of Hygienic Foods. Guided by his father's vision of delivering premium-quality milk to Punjab's consumers, Mr. Vinod Kumar Dutt embarked on this noble journey. Thus, Hygienic Foods (HF) was born, with a humble capacity of 25,000 liters of milk per day, laying the foundation for excellence in dairy.

ABOUT-US

Welcome to Chanakya Dairy Products Private Ltd.! Since 1994, we've been crafting dairy perfection under the esteemed HF Super brand. From our modest beginnings to becoming northern India's go-to for quality, freshness, and innovation, we've led the way. With visionary leadership from Mr. Vinod Kumar Dutt, we've revolutionized procurement, ensuring every drop of milk comes directly from trusted producers. In 2005, we expanded to Chanakya Dairy Products Private Ltd., setting new standards with a capacity of 250,000 liters per day. Today, boasting a processing capacity of 500,000 liters daily, we offer a diverse range of dairy delights, including pasteurized milk, butter, ghee, and irresistible ice cream flavors. Trusted nationally and internationally, HF Super is synonymous with purity and excellence. With a network of 5000 dedicated farmers and nine chilling centers, freshness is guaranteed. Our commitment to quality has earned us the trust of prestigious institutions and multinational clients. Join us at Chanakya Dairy, where every sip tells a story of uncompromising quality and unmatched freshness.
